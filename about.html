About Envision

Envision Architectural Consulting Engineers is one of Ajman’s growing design practices, offering comprehensive services in Architecture and Interior Design. Led by Chief Architects Smita Anand and Pawan Kumar, our team of skilled architects, interior designers, and civil and MEP engineers delivers innovative and inspiring solutions across residential, commercial, institutional, and healthcare projects.

Our projects are distinguished by their unique and high-quality architectural outcomes, reflecting our unwavering commitment to design excellence, research, and development. We take pride in our reputation for delivering superior design solutions, efficient project management, and successful project execution. From initial concept sketches to contract administration, we work diligently to ensure every project meets the highest standards of quality and client satisfaction.

Our Vision

ENVISION AEC has the vision to be a global leader in innovative architectural and engineering solutions, transforming the built environment through sustainable design, cutting-edge technology, and collaborative expertise. We are shaping communities and enhancing lives by creating functional, aesthetic, and environmentally responsible spaces that stand the test of time.

Team Envision

At Envision Architectural & Engineering Consultants, we take pride in the expertise and dedication of our diverse team, which plays a pivotal role in delivering exceptional engineering solutions.

Meet our experts

Architect Smita Anand

Founder & Managing Director

Ar. Smita Anand is the Managing Director of Envision, an esteemed engineering consulting firm specialising in architecture and interior design. With a Bachelor of Architecture in Building Construction and Management from a recognised university in India, she possesses a strong academic foundation that has fueled her successful career in the architecture field.

With over 8 years of experience in India and 4+ years of experience in the UAE, Ar. Smita Anand has honed her expertise in architecture design and interior solutions. Her extensive experience has enabled her to work on diverse projects across both countries, demonstrating a deep understanding of design principles, construction management, and the unique challenges of the industry.

As a leader, Ar. Smita brings a wealth of knowledge, creativity, and a commitment to delivering high-quality and sustainable solutions. Her vision drives the firm’s growth, ensuring that every project is executed with precision, innovation, and attention to detail.

Architect Pawan Kumar

Co-Founder & Lead Architect

Ar. Pawan Kumar is the Lead Architect at Envision, with over 15 years of experience in architecture and design. He completed his Bachelor of Architecture in Building Construction and Management from a recognised university in India, where he gained a strong theoretical and practical grounding in the field.

With more than 15 years of experience in India and over 6 years in the UAE, Ar. Pawan Kumar has become a key figure in architecture design & interior, landscape architecture, and residential buildings. His expertise in design and planning has allowed him to deliver exceptional, sustainable solutions tailored to meet the needs of diverse clients.

Ar. Pawan Kumar has played an instrumental role in the successful delivery of over 100+ villas and 50+ buildings in the UAE, showcasing his proficiency in both residential and commercial projects. His creativity, technical skills, and leadership ensure that every project is executed to the highest standards, from initial design concepts to completed structures.

As an expert in design and planning, Ar. Pawan Kumar is known for his ability to seamlessly blend aesthetics with functionality, ensuring that each project meets the client’s vision and requirements.
